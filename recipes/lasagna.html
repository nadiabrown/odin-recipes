Vegetarian Lasagna

The very best Vegetarian Lasagna! Walnuts, zucchini, carrots, onions, and mushrooms blitzed up into a meat-like texture that gets layered with lots of cheese and sauce. Cozy + nourishing!

Ingredients

  1. 4 garlic cloves
  2. 1 onion, peel and cut into chunks
  3. 2 medium carrots, cut into chunks
  4. 1 cup of fresh mushrooms
  5. 1 cup of walnuts
  6. 1 small zucchini, cut into small pieces
  7. 2 tablespoons of butter or olive oil
  8. 1 teaspoon of oregano
  9. 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  10. 1-3 teaspoons of kosher salt and black pepper
  11. 3 cups of tomato sauce
  12. 1 cup of mascarpone
  13. 2 cloves of garlic, grated
  14. 1 teaspoon of salt
  15. 2 cups of parmesean, shredded
  16. 2 1/2 cups of mozzerella, shredded
  17. 1 box of lasanga noodles
